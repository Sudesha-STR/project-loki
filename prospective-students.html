Prospective Students

If you are interested in PhD

If research intrigues you and your curiosity is never satisfied, and working on challenging task in keeping with the current technological progress is your passion, then PhD is the right path for you. Contact us with your SoP and detailed CV before you apply to let us know you are interested. The institute conducts interview twice a year for PhD admissions. Follow this link to apply for our PhD programs.

Internship Opportunities

If you are just looking for some experience and are willing to contribute to our ongoing projects, do reach out to us for summer internship opportunities.

Post-Doc & Project Staff Recruitment

If a doctorate degree has still not satisfied your thirst for research and academic, join us as a Post-Doc candidate. Share your detailed CV over email.

If you are not looking for long term commitment but want some hands on experience, you can join us as project staff. Share your detailed CV over email.
